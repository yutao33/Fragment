Welcome to DevOpen Controller Management GUI

You can get the following information from this GUI:

  • From "Controller Information", you can get the basic information of your controller.
  • From "NETWORK", you can get topology visualization view about your controller.
  • From "FAST", there are "Function Instance Table", "Function Instance Graph" and "Data Flow Graph" about the FAST system in your controller.
  • From "MAPLE", you can get the real-time trace tree visualization about your Maple App in your controller.
